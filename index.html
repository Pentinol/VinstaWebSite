Frequently Asked Questions

White screen on startup
Try turning on a VPN and restarting the program a few times.
Infinite login loading
You need to enable the internet and restart the program.
Infinite loading when importing a model
Your model is not suitable for rigging and conflicts with the program's algorithm.
Animations are applied incorrectly
Correctly place the key markers as indicated in the Auto-Rigger window and adjust the slider values.
